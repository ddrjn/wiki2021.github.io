Introduction

Throughout the year our team organized various workshops, scientific art exhibition, escape room, participated in many science fairs, and introduced the fundamentalprinciples of synthetic biology to a wider audience of different ages and fields.And we do not just do it, we do it in an entertaining and funny way. We do not teachwith complex over-the-head explanations, we teach while playing.
